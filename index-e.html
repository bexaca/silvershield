About us

Silver Shield is a private company based in Stara Pazova. Specializes in standard and innovative welding processes. A wide selection of tools and equipment allows us to provide high quality services we offer to our clients. We have experience in working with various types of metals and alloys, so we are in able to respond to all requests from our clients.

Our welding work is certified in accordance with ISO-3834-2 European international standard, created by welding professionals.

In order to better understand the needs of clients and meet their capabilities, we have formed a team whose knowledge of the domestic and foreign markets enables the improvement of the quality of services, technological innovation and increase the efficiency of the entire business of the company.

The business ethics of the company is based on the quality of services provided and work performed.

Services

MIG and MAG

  • MIG – Metal Inter Gas (welding of metals in internal gas)
  • MAG – Metal Active Gas (welding of metal in "active" gas)

The most common applications of MIG / MAG welding are in the welding of non-ferrous metals, thin sheets, high-alloy steel and other metals. It is an extremely fast, modern method, applicable to all types of metals in all positions.

TIG and WIG

  • TIG – Tungsten Inter Gas
  • WIG – Wolfram Inter Gas

Synonymous with argon welding. This welding method is precise and of high quality. In the welding process itself, the intense electric arc is calibrated to the material we want to weld. In this way, it is possible to join the base metal without or with the use of additional material, depending on needs.

MMA and REL

  • MMA – Manual Metal Arc
  • REL – Manual Electric Arc Welding

This method was first applied in Russia. In the welding process itself, coated electrodes are used, which have become synonymous with manual metal arc.
